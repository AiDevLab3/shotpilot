## The Problem

Current AI filmmaking workflows suffer from three critical gaps:

**1. The Pre-Production Gap**  
Users jump directly into production without a clear creative vision, leading to vague prompts, style inconsistency, and wasted compute on shots that don't match their intent.

**2. The Consistency Gap**  
Character identity drifts across shots. Lighting changes. Style shifts. The final output feels disjointed because there's no **Project DNA** guiding the entire production.

**3. The Quality Control Gap**  
Most platforms offer binary pass/fail feedback. Users don't know *why* an output failed or *how* to fix it. They regenerate blindly, hoping for better results.

---

## The Solution: A 5-Agent Film Crew

Cine-AI operates as a **specialized film production team**, not a single AI. Each agent has a distinct role, expertise, and responsibility:

### 1. **Creative Director** (Pre-Production)
- Collaborates with you to formulate a cohesive creative vision
- Asks clarifying questions about theme, style, tone, and mood
- Develops script and shot list through adaptive workflow
- Creates **Project DNA** that guides the entire production
- Adapts to your workflow: has script / vague idea / detailed vision

**Output**: Project DNA Document, Script, Shot List, Style Guide

### 2. **Chief of Staff** (Producer)
- Maintains Project DNA (character SoulID, style, narrative intent)
- Orchestrates sub-agent collaboration
- Enforces protocols (3-Strike Rule, Shot-Lock gates)
- Makes pivot decisions when primary model fails

**Output**: Production coordination, protocol enforcement, pivot authority

### 3. **Director of Photography** (DP)
- Expert in image model syntax across 11 models
- Designs composition, focal length, aperture, and optical specs
- Generates "Hero Frame" stills (master images)
- Provides optical metadata for temporal consistency

**Output**: Hero Frames with technical metadata

### 4. **Holistic Image Auditor** (Quality Control)
- Comprehensive image analysis across **6 dimensions**:
  - Physics (lighting, shadows, perspective)
  - Style Consistency (aesthetic, tone, mood)
  - Lighting & Atmosphere (artistic intent, motivated sources)
  - Clarity (sharpness, focus, depth of field)
  - Objects & Composition (placement, scale, relationships)
  - Character Identity (appearance, clothing, features)
- Provides detailed feedback and actionable suggestions
- **3-tier recommendation framework**:
  - **LOCK IT IN** (95-100%): Proceed to next phase
  - **REFINE** (70-94%): Specific improvements needed
  - **REGENERATE** (0-69%): Start over with corrected prompt

**Output**: Detailed analysis reports with specific recommendations

### 5. **Motion Agent** (Lead Editor)
- Expert in video model syntax across 11 models
- Ensures 24fps motion cadence and physics-based camera moves
- Receives optical metadata from DP for temporal consistency
- Animates approved Hero Frames using image-to-video workflow

**Output**: Final video clips with motion and camera movement
