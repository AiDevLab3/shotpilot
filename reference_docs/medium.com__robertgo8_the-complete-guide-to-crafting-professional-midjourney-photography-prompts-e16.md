## üîß Prompt Structure Framework
### Essential Components

[SUBJECT] + [PHOTOGRAPHY STYLE] + [TECHNICAL DETAILS] + [LIGHTING] + [COMPOSITION] + [ATMOSPHERE] + [CAMERA SETTINGS] + [MIDJOURNEY PARAMETERS]

### Detailed Breakdown
1. **Subject Description** (What)
   - Main subject with specific details
   - Secondary elements and context
   - Human characteristics (age, expression, clothing)
2. **Photography Style** (Genre)
   - Portrait, Landscape, Street, Fashion, Product, Architectural, Documentary, etc.
   - Artistic movement references (if applicable)
3. **Technical Details** (Equipment)
   - Camera type (DSLR, Medium Format, Film Camera)
   - Lens specifications (24mm wide-angle, 85mm portrait, 200mm telephoto)
   - Film type (Kodak Portra 400, Fujifilm Velvia 50)
4. **Lighting Setup** (How)
   - Natural light (golden hour, blue hour, overcast, harsh midday)
   - Artificial light (studio lighting, rim light, backlight, soft box)
   - Light quality (soft, hard, diffused, dramatic)
5. **Composition** (Framing)
   - Rule of thirds, leading lines, symmetry, negative space
   - Camera angle (eye-level, low-angle, bird's eye view, worm's eye view)
   - Depth of field (shallow DoF, deep focus)
6. **Atmosphere & Mood** (Feel)
   - Emotional tone (melancholic, joyful, mysterious, serene)
   - Color grading (warm tones, cool palette, desaturated, high contrast)
   - Weather conditions (foggy, rainy, sunny, stormy)
7. **Camera Settings** (Technical)
   - Aperture (f/1.4 for bokeh, f/16 for landscape)
   - Shutter speed (long exposure, freeze motion)
   - ISO implications (film grain, clean image)
8. **Midjourney Parameters** (Optimization)
   - `--ar` (aspect ratio: 16:9, 4:5, 1:1)
   - `--style` (raw, scenic)
   - `--stylize` (0-1000, controls artistic interpretation)
   - `--chaos` (0-100, variation control)
   - `--quality` (0.25, 0.5, 1, 2)
