Frequently asked questions
What is Runway 4.5 and how is it different from previous versions?

Runway 4.5 is the latest text-to-video AI model from Runway. It is a significant advancement from older versions like Gen-2, offering improved realism, physical accuracy, and consistency in the videos it generates. It is currently ranked as a top model on independent benchmarks for its quality.

Can I use my own images or videos with Runway 4.5?

Not right at launch, but Runway has confirmed that features like Image-to-Video and Video-to-Video are coming soon to Runway 4.5. These updates will allow you to use your own media as a reference to guide the AI, giving you more control over characters and styles.

How much does it cost to use Runway 4.5?

Access to Runway 4.5 is included in all of Runway's paid subscription plans, starting with the Standard plan at $12 per user/month (when billed annually). The cost of generating video is based on a credit system, which is comparable to the cost of the previous Gen-2 model.

What are the main limitations of Runway 4.5?

Runway 4.5 still has some limitations. The company has noted challenges with causal reasoning (an effect appearing before its cause) and object permanence (objects disappearing randomly). They are actively working to improve these areas.

Is Runway 4.5 suitable for professional filmmakers?

With its high-quality output, cinematic controls, and upcoming features like keyframing, Runway 4.5 is a tool for professional filmmakers, animators, and content creators looking to speed up their workflow or create visuals that would be difficult or expensive to produce traditionally.

How do I get access to Runway 4.5?

Runway is rolling out access to Runway 4.5 gradually to its users. It will be available on all paid plans. If you're a subscriber and don't see it yet, it should appear in your account soon.
