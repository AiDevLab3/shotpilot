# Master Prompt Template: Cinematic Style Examples

**By Manus AI** | January 26, 2026

---

Here are three distinct examples of the Master Prompt Template filled out for different cinematic styles. Each example demonstrates how to tailor the template to achieve a specific genre aesthetic, from the grand scale of high fantasy to the intimate charm of a classic romantic comedy.

---

## Example 1: The High-Fantasy Epic

### **1. Core Concept & Style**

*   **High-Level Concept:** A cinematic still from a high-fantasy epic film.
*   **Director/Artist Style:** In the style of Peter Jackson.
*   **Overall Mood & Atmosphere:** An awe-inspiring, majestic scene with a sense of ancient wonder.

### **2. Setting & Environment**

*   **Location:** The edge of a cliff in a colossal, mist-filled valley, with impossibly tall, waterfall-covered mountains in the far distance and the ruins of a forgotten city below.
*   **Time of Day & Weather:** Early morning, as the first rays of sunlight break through the clouds, illuminating the mist.

### **3. The Subject**

*   **Subject Description:** A stoic, female elven warrior with sharp, elegant features and long, silver hair.
*   **Pose & Action:** She stands poised and alert, one hand resting on the hilt of her sword as she gazes out over the valley.
*   **Facial Expression & Emotion:** with an expression of solemn duty and quiet strength.

### **4. Wardrobe & Textures**

*   **Clothing:** She is wearing intricately crafted, dark green leather armor, adorned with silver leaf motifs. The armor shows subtle signs of wear, with minor scuffs and scratches from past battles. A heavy, deep blue cloak with a fur-lined collar is draped over her shoulders.
*   **Skin & Hair Texture:** Her skin is fair and appears almost luminous in the soft light, with a subtle, natural texture. Her silver hair is woven into a complex braid, with a few loose strands catching the gentle breeze.

### **5. The Camera Rig (Technical Details)**

*   **Camera Body:** Shot on an ARRI Alexa 65.
*   **Lens:** Using a Panavision Primo 70 series lens.
*   **Focal Length:** 24mm.
*   **Aperture:** with an aperture of f/11 for a deep, expansive depth of field.

### **6. Composition & Framing**

*   **Shot Type:** An extreme wide shot.
*   **Camera Angle:** from a slightly low angle to emphasize the scale of the mountains and the heroic stature of the warrior.
*   **Compositional Rules:** The composition follows the rule of thirds, with the warrior placed on the right vertical line, looking into the vast, open space on the left.

### **7. Lighting & Color**

*   **Lighting Setup:** The scene is lit with natural, early morning light. The sun acts as a backlight, creating a powerful golden rim light around the warrior's silhouette.
*   **Advanced Lighting Effects:** Featuring volumetric light shafts (God rays) piercing through the clouds and illuminating the mist in the valley below.
*   **Color Palette:** The image has a rich, natural color palette with deep greens, earthy browns, and cool blues, contrasted with the warm golden light of the sunrise.

### **8. Negative Prompts**

*   **Exclusions:** Avoid oversaturated or cartoonish colors, flat lighting, digital artifacts, or modern-looking equipment.

### **9. Model-Specific Parameters**

*   **Parameters:** `--ar 21:9 --style raw --v 6.0`

## Example 2: The Gritty Cyberpunk Scene

### **1. Core Concept & Style**

*   **High-Level Concept:** A cinematic still from a gritty, dystopian cyberpunk film.
*   **Director/Artist Style:** In the style of Ridley Scott's "Blade Runner."
*   **Overall Mood & Atmosphere:** A dark, oppressive, and rain-soaked atmosphere, punctuated by vibrant neon lights.

### **2. Setting & Environment**

*   **Location:** A crowded, narrow street market in a futuristic, overpopulated metropolis. The ground is littered with discarded electronics and refuse.
*   **Time of Day & Weather:** Perpetual night, with a constant, drizzling rain that makes the streets slick and reflective.

### **3. The Subject**

*   **Subject Description:** A cynical, cybernetically enhanced bounty hunter.
*   **Pose & Action:** He is leaning against a food stall, surrounded by steam from a noodle vendor, while discreetly scanning the crowd.
*   **Facial Expression & Emotion:** with a detached, weary expression, his eyes partially obscured by the shadow of his collar.

### **4. Wardrobe & Textures**

*   **Clothing:** He is wearing a high-collared, black synthetic jacket with a worn, matte texture and multiple pockets. The jacket is wet from the rain, with visible water droplets beading on the surface. One of his arms is a complex, exposed cybernetic prosthetic made of dark, unpolished metal.
*   **Skin & Hair Texture:** His visible skin is pale and has a slightly clammy texture. His hair is short, dark, and matted from the rain.

### **5. The Camera Rig (Technical Details)**

*   **Camera Body:** Shot on a RED V-Raptor.
*   **Lens:** Using a Hawk V-lite Anamorphic lens.
*   **Focal Length:** 50mm.
*   **Aperture:** with an aperture of f/2.8 to isolate the subject from the chaotic background.

### **6. Composition & Framing**

*   **Shot Type:** A medium shot.
*   **Camera Angle:** from an eye-level perspective, creating a sense of immediacy and immersion in the crowd.
*   **Compositional Rules:** The composition is dense and cluttered, with the subject framed by the steam and the surrounding market-goers, creating a claustrophobic feel.

### **7. Lighting & Color**

*   **Lighting Setup:** The scene is lit almost exclusively by the chaotic, multi-colored glow of holographic advertisements and neon signs from the surrounding shops.
*   **Advanced Lighting Effects:** Featuring prominent anamorphic lens flare from the bright neon lights, and caustic reflections from the puddles on the ground.
*   **Color Palette:** The image has a high-contrast color palette, with deep, inky blacks and vibrant, saturated colors like electric blue, magenta, and acid green.

### **8. Negative Prompts**

*   **Exclusions:** Avoid clean or sterile environments, bright or natural daylight, and overly heroic poses.

### **9. Model-Specific Parameters**

*   **Parameters:** `--ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6.0`

## Example 3: The 1950s Romantic Comedy

### **1. Core Concept & Style**

*   **High-Level Concept:** A film grab from a classic 1950s romantic comedy.
*   **Director/Artist Style:** In the style of Billy Wilder.
*   **Overall Mood & Atmosphere:** A lighthearted, charming, and optimistic atmosphere.

### **2. Setting & Environment**

*   **Location:** A bustling, sun-drenched cafe in Paris during the spring.
*   **Time of Day & Weather:** A bright, sunny afternoon.

### **3. The Subject**

*   **Subject Description:** A witty, elegantly dressed American woman in her late 20s.
*   **Pose & Action:** She is sitting at a small, round cafe table, laughing candidly as she listens to someone just out of frame.
*   **Facial Expression & Emotion:** with a genuine, radiant smile and sparkling eyes.

### **4. Wardrobe & Textures**

*   **Clothing:** She is wearing a classic 1950s-style A-line dress made of a light, polka-dotted cotton fabric. She also wears a pair of short, white gloves and a small, chic hat.
*   **Skin & Hair Texture:** Her skin is smooth with a soft, natural complexion. Her hair is perfectly coiffed in a stylish 1950s bob.

### **5. The Camera Rig (Technical Details)**

*   **Camera Body:** Shot on a Panavision Panaflex camera with Eastman Kodak 5247 film stock.
*   **Lens:** Using a Cooke Panchro/Classic lens.
*   **Focal Length:** 50mm.
*   **Aperture:** with an aperture of f/4 for a soft, pleasing depth of field.

### **6. Composition & Framing**

*   **Shot Type:** A medium close-up shot.
*   **Camera Angle:** from a slightly high angle, creating a flattering and endearing perspective.
*   **Compositional Rules:** The composition is balanced and centered, focusing entirely on the subject's joyful expression.

### **7. Lighting & Color**

*   **Lighting Setup:** The scene is lit with bright, soft, natural light, as if from a large window or open-air seating. The lighting is high-key, with minimal shadows.
*   **Advanced Lighting Effects:** Featuring a subtle, warm glow that gives the scene a romantic and nostalgic feel.
*   **Color Palette:** The image has a vibrant, Technicolor-inspired palette with rich, saturated colors, particularly reds and blues, against a warm, creamy background.

### **8. Negative Prompts**

*   **Exclusions:** Avoid harsh shadows, modern clothing or hairstyles, desaturated colors, and gritty textures.

### **9. Model-Specific Parameters**

*   **Parameters:** `--ar 3:2 --style raw --v 6.0`
